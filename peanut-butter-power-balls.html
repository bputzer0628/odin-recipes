Peanut Butter Power Balls

Healthy Treat For On The Go

These protein filled balls of delight are a healthy choice that tastes like a treat. They may taste like cookie dough, but the are filled with nutritious ingredients such as flaxseed, chia seed, and oats.

This is a simple no bake recipe that is guaranteed to delight your taste buds and give you evergy to get through the day!

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • butter
  • heavy cream
  • parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce
